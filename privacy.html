Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Information We Collect

Pintly collects account information, beer logs, group memberships, photos you choose to upload, approximate check-in location details, reactions, reports, and device push tokens when notifications are enabled.

How We Use Information

We use this information to operate beer logging, groups, leaderboards, venue suggestions, photo storage, safety features, support, abuse prevention, and app reliability.

Location, Camera, And Photos

Location, camera, and photo library access are requested only when you create or edit Pintly content. You can change these permissions in device settings.

Sharing

Group posts are visible to people who can access the relevant group. Blocking a user hides their posts from your view but does not remove their contributions from group totals.

Deletion

You can request account deletion inside Pintly. Deletion removes or anonymizes account data where required for app operation, safety, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

For privacy requests, contact Pintly at nade.million.beers@gmail.com.
